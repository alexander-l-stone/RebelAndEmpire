Hack Communications:
Cost: 1
Effect: Roll Agent Skill. On success you know the Imperial Sector Action that will happen this turn. On Failure, nothing happens.

Incite Rebellion:
Cost: 10
Effect: Attempt to incite rebellion on an Imperial Planet. Roll Agents skill at 1 extra difficulty for every 10 units on the planet with Garrison. Levels are Loyal > Content > Conflicted > Disloyal > Rebelling. If a planet goes from Rebelling to Free, you immediately get 1d10 Militia on the planet who immediately fight any Imperial Land Garrison. If you win, the planet is yours, otherwise it stays Rebelling.

Smuggle Goods:
Cost: 0
Effect: Target an Imperial planet. Roll Agents skill. On success gain Credits equal to its Trade Value. On failure, nothing happens.
